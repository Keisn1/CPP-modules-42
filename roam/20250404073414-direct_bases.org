#+title: direct and indirect bases
#+date: [2025-04-04 Fri 07:34]
#+startup: overview

- bases that appear after the colon in a class declarations are called *direct*
- the list after the colon of a class-declaration is called *base-clause*
- so classes that appear inside the base-clause are *direct*

- base classes of direct bases are called *indirect*
